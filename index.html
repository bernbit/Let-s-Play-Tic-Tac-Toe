HOW TO PLAY THE GAME?

Taking Turns

  • Player "X" goes first, followed by player "O."
  • Each player takes turns placing their mark in an empty square on the grid.

Winning the Game

  • The game ends when one player gets three of their marks in a row.
  • A row can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  • If all squares are filled, and no player has won, the game is a tie
